Phase 6 — First-visit Landing UX Rework.

Do NOT modify penalty gameplay.
Do NOT modify Football IQ logic/questions.
Do NOT modify progression economy.
Do NOT modify FanCard design.
Do NOT modify card export/share logic.
Do NOT modify quiz scoring.
Do NOT modify Firestore rules.

Problem:
The landing page currently looks nice, but first-time users see too many choices immediately:
- Quiz
- Penalty
- IQ
- Matchday
- Groups
- Take Quiz
- Sign In
- Already have a result
- identity cards

A new user from TikTok does not immediately understand the official flow.

Product decision:
We have two flows:

1. Guest acquisition flow:
Landing → Start quiz → Result → Preview card

2. Official retention flow:
Sign in → Official Fan Card → Football IQ → Penalty Queen → Stars/Badges → Share upgraded card

We should NOT force login before the quiz.
But Sign In / Official Card must be more visible and understandable.

Goal:
Make the landing page clearly guide first-time users.

---

## 1. Simplify guest header

On the landing page only, for non-authenticated/guest users:

Show:
- Fangirl FC logo
- Start Quiz button
- Sign In button

Hide or de-emphasize the large nav pill row:
- Quiz
- Penalty
- IQ
- Matchday
- Groups

Reason:
The nav row creates too many choices before the user understands the product.

If removing completely is risky, hide it only on the landing hero area or move it below the hero after the “How it works” section.

Do not remove TopNav globally from the app.
Only adjust the landing page experience.

---

## 2. Update hero copy

Current hero is too focused only on the quiz.

New hero direction:

Headline:
“Get World Cup Ready”

Second line:
“Find your Fangirl Type”

Subtitle:
“Take the quiz, create your Fan Card, learn football, play Penalty Queen, and upgrade your level.”

Primary CTA:
“Start free — Take the quiz”

Secondary CTA:
“Sign in to save my official card”

CTA behavior:
- Primary goes to /quiz
- Secondary goes to current sign-in flow

Do not force sign-in before quiz.

---

## 3. Add clear two-flow explanation

Add a compact section near the hero, before identity cards:

Title:
“How Fangirl FC works”

Show 5 steps:
1. Take the quiz
2. Create your Fan Card
3. Sign in to make it official
4. Learn football with Football IQ
5. Play Penalty Queen and upgrade your card

Keep this mobile-first and visually clean.
Use small cards or a vertical stepper.

---

## 4. Clarify Official Card concept

Add a small premium info box:

Title:
“Make it official”

Copy:
“You can play first. Sign in when you want to save your official card, keep your stars, and share your progress.”

CTA:
“Sign in to save progress”

This should be visible, but secondary to “Start free”.

---

## 5. Fix ambiguous existing CTA

Replace:
“Already have a result? Make my card”

With:
“I already played — continue to my card”

or:
“Already have a result? Continue to my card”

Make it visually secondary.

---

## 6. Do not break current flows

Preserve:
- /quiz
- /result
- /card
- /football-iq
- /penalty
- TopNav on internal pages
- card export
- share/save logic
- auth flow

---

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- First-time landing clearly explains what the app does.
- Primary action is obvious: Start free / Take the quiz.
- Sign in is visible but not forced.
- User understands that the official card is saved after sign in.
- The flow Quiz → Card → IQ → Penalty → Share is clear.
- Landing has fewer competing CTAs above the fold.
- Mobile layout is clean.
- Typecheck passes.
- Build passes.
- No console errors.
- Report exact files changed.
