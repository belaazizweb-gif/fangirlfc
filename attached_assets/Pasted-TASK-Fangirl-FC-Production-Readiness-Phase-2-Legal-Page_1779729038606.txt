- images are processed in the browser for the card creator
- background removal runs client-side in the browser

or

- fan identity
- stars/progression
- challenge completion

State accurately:
- sign-in is optional where available
- Firebase Auth may process email, display name, photoURL, UID depending on sign-in method
- Firestore may store public/shared card metadata, display name, team, fan identity, ranking/leaderboard data

If no real analytics SDK exists, state:
“Fangirl FC does not currently use a production analytics SDK. Some technical logs may appear during development.”

Do not claim analytics if not implemented.

6. Sharing
Explain:
- shared links may display selected card/profile information publicly to anyone with the link
- users should avoid sharing personal information they do not want public

Use cautious wording:
- Fangirl FC is intended for general football fans and is not directed at children under 13
- users under the required age in their country should use the app with permission from a parent/guardian

8. No sale of personal data
State:
- Fangirl FC does not sell personal data.

- Firebase / Google services if configured

support@fangirlfc.live

PHASE 3 — CREATE /support PAGE
==================================================

Create /support.

Must include:

Title:
Support

Contact:
support@fangirlfc.live

FAQ sections:

1. How do I create a fan card?
Answer:
Go to Creator, choose a template, upload a photo, customize your card, then download/export it.

2. How does background removal work?
Answer:
It runs in the browser using client-side AI. First use may download model data.

3. Why does background removal take time the first time?
Answer:
The browser may need to download AI model data before processing.

4. Does Fangirl FC upload my image?
Answer:
Card creation images are processed in the browser and are not intentionally uploaded to Fangirl FC servers for the creator flow.

5. How do I download/export my card?
Answer:
Use the export/download button in the creator. If it fails, try Chrome, update the browser, or clear storage.

6. Is Fangirl FC official?
Answer:
No. It is fan-made and not affiliated with FIFA, EA, EA SPORTS FC, UEFA, clubs, leagues, federations, or sponsors.

7. How do I delete local saved data?
Answer:
Clear browser site data for fangirlfc.live or use browser settings.

- /terms
- /creator
