Current product concern:

This may be fine for fun/social card generation, but it must not break the official identity logic used for progression, challenges, stars, unlocked identities, and official card status.

We need to clearly separate:

1. baseQuizIdentity
- the identity calculated from the quiz
- should be used for official result, progression, stars, challenges, leaderboard, unlocked identities
- should not be casually overwritten by card customization

2. activeCardIdentity
- the identity currently displayed on the visual card
- can be customized for fun card generation
- used for normal card preview/export/share
- should be treated as a visual/card variation, not necessarily the official quiz identity
